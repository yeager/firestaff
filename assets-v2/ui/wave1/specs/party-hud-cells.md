# V2 Wave 1 Spec: Party HUD Cells

## Scope
- Standard slot/hand/inventory framed cell family
- No item icons in this pack

## Canonical sizes
- 4K master: **216×216**
- 1080p derived: **108×108**

## Source reference
- DM1 slot box family from graphics **0033, 0034, 0035**

## Art direction stub
- Small framed cell with strong readability
- Slight material wear is welcome
- Must stay crisp after 50% downscale

## Guardrails
- Design for repeated use in party HUD contexts
- Keep border depth readable at 1080p
- Avoid overfilling the interior; item/icon art will sit here later
