## Main Quest: Stop Lord Dragoth's Invasion

The overarching objective is to prevent **Lord Dragoth** from using Skullkeep as a beachhead
for an invasion of the world **Zalk** from another dimension.

**Ultimate goal:** Fix the **Zo Link** machine, cross into the **Void**, and defeat Dragoth.

---

## Quest Phases & Objectives

### Phase 1: Prologue / Hall of Champions
**Objective:** Select your starting party

- Enter the **Hall of Champions** (cave with cryo-stasis machines)
- View the inventories and statistics of 15 available champions
- Choose **three champions** to accompany mandatory protagonist **Torham Zed**
- Aim for a balanced party: fighters in front, magic-makers behind

---

### Phase 2: Entry Stage / Tutorial Area
**Objective:** Learn mechanics, gather basic supplies

- Move the table blocking the sun-burst picture
- Collect: key, money box, **magic map** (best new feature in DM2)
- Find healing solutions in nearby chest
- Locate the **resurrection altar** (for reviving dead champions)
- Head outside to the shop area

---

### Phase 3: Shop Area & Preparation
**Objective:** Stock up on essential supplies before entering Skullkeep

- Visit all shops: weapons, food, general supplies
- **Critical purchases:**
  - **Botas** (water carriers) — one per character, essential
  - **Compass** — navigation aid
  - **Rope** — equip to go down holes
  - Swords/axes for fighters (best damage against monsters)
- Sell excess items at the left shop table
- Exchange coins for gems (place money box on it)
- Search the **fountain** for money
- Stock up heavily on **food** — Skullkeep has less food available

---

### Phase 4: Overworld — Collect the Four Keys
**Objective:** Find the four pieces of the blue clan key needed to open Skullkeep's main gate

The green door at Skullkeep (with two golden dragons) requires **four pieces of the blue clan key**.

**Key piece locations:**
- **The \* key** — on a table in the food shop; opens the \* gate in the overworld
- **Clan key pieces** — found across the overworld zones (Misty area stonehenge has one)
- Explore all areas: bat caves, misty area, lightning area, brown monster area

**Zone exploration order:**
1. Go through the **\* gate** (requires \* key)
2. Enter the **bat caves** — green gem, sellable bat remains
3. Explore the **brown monster area** — worms drop worm food
4. Enter the **misty area** via archway — find stonehenge with lightning key, scout map, one clan key piece
5. Proceed to the **lightning area** — beware of red bearded men who steal weapons

---

### Phase 5: Enter Skullkeep
**Objective:** Enter the fortress and navigate to the Zo Link

- Use the four key pieces on the green door (two golden dragons)
- Navigate the multi-level fortress (outdoor + building + indoor dungeon levels)
- Press switches, pull levers, step on pressure plates; fight monsters

---

### Phase 6: Find and Repair the Zo Link
**Objective:** Locate the Zo Link machine and restore its function

- Find the ancient **Zo Link** machine in the depths of Skullkeep
- Repair/restore the machine using items or actions discovered in the dungeon

---

### Phase 7: Cross into the Void
**Objective:** Activate the Zo Link and enter Dragoth's dimension

- Activate the repaired Zo Link → cross into the **Void** (Dragoth's home dimension)
- Final dungeon level (DUNGEON_LEVEL_14)

---

### Phase 8: Confront and Defeat Lord Dragoth
**Objective:** End the threat

- Fight **Lord Dragoth** (AI index 30)
  - Huge, blue with red tabard and big teeth
  - Casts Reflector spell (0x55); summons Dragoth Attack Minions (AI type 34)
- **Strategy:** Use the **Numenstaff** to deal damage; when health drops, teleport out to heal, return and repeat
- Defeating Dragoth triggers the endgame sequence

---

### Phase 9: Face Lord Delos (Post-Dragoth)
**Objective:** Complete the game

- After Dragoth is defeated, **Lord Delos** appears
- Delos turns Dragoth into an insect ("yum yum")
- Lord Delos then challenges the party to a "re-match"
- **The game ends with this sequel hook**

## Side Quests & Activities

### Magic Maps
- **Scout map** — produces a large eye that scouts the area; reveals enemies, secret rooms, mana items, projectiles
- **Magic porter map** — teleports party members
- **Magic fighter map** — summons a fighter ally

### Shop Economy
- Buy/sell weapons, armor, food, supplies
- Exchange coins for gems (left table in shops)
- Cannot defeat the shop guard — don't try

---

## Win Conditions

1. Activate Zo Link after repairing it
2. Cross into the Void
3. Defeat Lord Dragoth
4. Survive Lord Delos (apparent final challenge / sequel hook)
