# DM2 V1 — Bonus Content / Special Editions

## Known Releases
- Standard edition: DOS floppy version (Interplay, 1994)
- Windows 3.1 version: Included in some distributions with VP6 video codec

## No Pre-Order Bonuses Found
- DM2 predates the pre-order bonus culture of modern gaming
- No known exclusive items, dungeons, or character classes

## No Special Editions
- No CollectorsEdition
- No GOTYEdition 
- No DemoDiscs with exclusive content

## Shareware/Freeware
- NOT shareware - sold as full retail product
- No free release versions

## What Was Included
- 6 floppy disks with game
- Manual with dungeon maps
- Some packages included strategy guides

## Related Content
- Soul of a Robot novelization (separate product)
- Official strategy guide published

## Conclusion
DM2 was a straightforward retail release with no variant bonus content.
No special editions, no pre-order exclusives, no bonus material.
